Merlin's magic URL
------------------

Here are the URL to get the princess room temperature on a specific timestamp:
https://emoncms.fr/feed/value.json?id=77&time=1672527600

- Use the date command to get the timestamp for this date: Wednesday 6 September 2023 at midnight.
- Then, use this timestamp in the url passed to the curl command to get the temperature and save it in the drawer.
